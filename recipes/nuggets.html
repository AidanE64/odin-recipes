Chick-Fil-A nuggs recipe:

Back to recipes

Stolen from u/bluewaterlilies who stole it from someone else

Ingredients:

Nuggets

  • 6 chicken breasts
  • 2 & 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 cup pickle juice
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 & 1/2 cups flour
  • 6 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 6 tsp salt
  • 3 tsp pepper

Sauce

  • 1 cup mayo
  • 4 tsp mustard
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 4 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp hickory bbq sauce
  • paprika
  • mix all

Instructions:

  1. Whisk egg, milk, & pickle juice. Marinate 1 inch cubes of chicken 2-4 hrs in fridge
  2. Combine flour, powdered sugar, salt, and pepper in a ziplock. Shake. Add the chicken into the bag and coat them.
  3. Heat Peanut oil (about 1.5 inches) in skillet. Flour dropped in should sizzle, not pop.
  4. Fry 3-4 minutes on each side. Drain on paper towels.
