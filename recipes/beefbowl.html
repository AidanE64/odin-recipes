Korean Beef Bowl:

Back to recipes

Youtube/video tutorial

Ingredients:

  • 400g Sliced Sirloin Strips
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Onion
  • 1/4 apple
  • 1 Kiwi
  • 1/2 tsp ginger paste
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp white wine
  • 1/3 cup (80ml) Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Corn Starch
  • 1/2 tsp Sesame Oil

Instructions:

  1. Knuckle the beef with back of knife
  2. Slice Beef into small pieces
  3. Beat garlic, kiwi, apple, and onion w/ a food processor
  4. pour puree, ginger paste, honey, sugar, white wine, soy sauce, corn starch, and sesame oil into the meat bowl and mix
  5. Marinate meat for 30 min
  6. Add oil to pan. Sautee sliced onion until golden, then add marinated meat and cook
  7. Serve with rice and sprinkle some white sesame and chopped green onion if ur fancy
